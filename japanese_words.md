# Useful Japanese words

* Hello - Konninchiwa
* Thankyou - Arigato
* Thankyou (polite) - Arigato gosaimas
* Good morning - Ohayo
* Good morning (polite) - Ohayo Gosaimas
* Good evening - konbanva
* Welcome - toiitashimashide
* Good night - oyasuminasai
* bye - jaane
* Good bye - sayonara
* Telephone - Denva
* Italy - Itaria
* The UK - Igirisu
* France - Furaansu
* Teacher - Sensei
* Student - Gaksei
